About Us

Empowering Tomorrow is a passionate volunteering organization focused on contributing towards sustainability, education and advocacy. Through our outreach programs in underprivileged schools, we create a sense of environmental awareness and curiosity in science which will enable them to use science as a tool to find solutions for environmental problems around them

In our workshops, we promote concept-based learning through hands-on experimentation thus empowering these kids to be future leaders who are environmentally conscious, rational thinkers and problem solvers with a scientific temperament.

Our Mission

Our mission is to instill environmental consciousness and ignite curiosity among young minds empowering them to create a sustainable future and helping them develop a scientific approach to life.
